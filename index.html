African Students Asociation In Mersin

African Students Association in Mersin ( ASAM ) has been created in November 2013 by the gathering of 17 African students who came to study in Mersin. Until today, Our Association continues to maintain the characteristics of a warm family atmosphere for approximately 300 African students. The main goals of our association is to unite the students and strengthen the social bond amoung them and facilate their integration to the university and to the Turkish society. Organize the academic and social activities to improve themselves in many fields and also create good relationship with others associations and institutions.
